# Mindora - AI-Enhanced Career Guidance System

**Mindora** is a comprehensive, AI-powered career guidance platform designed to help students and professionals discover personalized career pathways based on their aptitude, personality, skills, and aspirations.

## 🚀 Features

### 1. **Aptitude Assessment**
- Comprehensive tests covering Mathematics, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Analytical skills
- Real-time scoring and detailed results
- AI-powered career recommendations based on test performance

### 2. **Personality Analysis**
- Multi-dimensional personality assessment
- Evaluation of traits like leadership, creativity, teamwork, and emotional stability
- Personality-based career matching

### 3. **Career Counselor Marketplace**
- Connect with verified industry experts from top companies (TCS, Wipro, Microsoft, etc.)
- View counselor profiles with ratings, reviews, and LinkedIn integration
- Example mentor: **Rohit Mahto** - Senior Data Scientist at TCS
- Filter counselors by field and specialization
- Book 1-on-1 sessions with professionals

### 4. **English Preparation**
- **Voice Chat Practice**: Conversational AI for spoken English improvement
- **Grammar Games**: Fill-in-the-blanks and sentence correction exercises
- **Word Games**: Word scramble puzzles with hints
- **Writing Practice**: Essay writing with AI feedback

### 5. **Resume Builder with ATS Scoring**
- Professional resume templates
- **ATS Score Analysis**: Analyze your resume against job descriptions
- Auto-optimization based on job posting links
- Unlimited resume creation for Premium users (Free tier: 3 resumes)
- Integration with GitHub and LinkedIn profiles

### 6. **Job Application Tracker**
- Track all your job applications in one place
- **Smart Email Filtering**: Auto-filter job-related emails to prevent missing important updates
- Application status tracking (Applied, Interview, Offer, Rejected)
- Store job links and interview notes

### 7. **Community Q&A**
- Ask and answer career-related questions
- Learn from peer experiences
- Pre-loaded with important questions about CGPA, placements, career transitions
- Upvoting and discussion threads

### 8. **College Search**
- Search best colleges by program and location
- Detailed information: Fees, Placement rates, Rankings
- **Featured Colleges**: Sponsored listings appear first
- Filter by fields like Data Science, Computer Science, etc.
- Includes top institutions: IIT Bombay, IIT Delhi, BITS Pilani, NITs

### 9. **Coding IDE for DSA Practice**
- **Daily DSA Questions** for placement preparation
- Built-in code editor with syntax highlighting
- Practice problems categorized by difficulty (Easy, Medium, Hard)
- Track your coding submissions and progress
- Hints and examples for each problem

### 10. **Job Postings Window**
- Daily updated job listings from top companies
- Filter by experience level, location, and salary
- Direct application links
- Internship and full-time opportunities

### 11. **Premium Features**
- Unlimited resume creation
- Portfolio management
- Advanced job alerts
- Priority access to counselors
- Upgrade for ₹999/month (demo pricing)

## 📊 Dataset

The platform includes a **comprehensive 200-row dataset** designed for training AI/ML models for career guidance. The dataset contains:

### Dataset Features:
- **User Profile**: Name, Age, Gender, Education Level, Field of Study, CGPA, College Tier
- **Aptitude Scores**: Mathematics, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, Analytical, Spatial Reasoning
- **Personality Traits**: Extraversion, Conscientiousness, Openness, Agreeableness, Emotional Stability, Leadership, Creativity, Team Orientation
- **Skills & Experience**: Programming Skills, Years of Experience, Internships, Projects, Certifications, Hackathons
- **Aspirations**: Career Goals, Industry Preferences, Work-Life Balance Priority, Salary Expectations, Relocation Willingness
- **Target Variables**: Recommended Career Path, Career Fit Score, Success Probability, Skill Gaps, Recommended Courses, Expected Salary Range

### Dataset Highlights:
- **200 diverse profiles** representing Indian students and professionals
- Realistic data with proper correlations (e.g., higher CGPA → better career outcomes)
- Indian context: Names, colleges (IIT, NIT, BITS), salary in INR (LPA)
- Multiple career fields: Data Science, Software Engineering, Product Management, Civil Engineering, Business Analysis, etc.

**View the complete dataset in `/dataset.md`**
